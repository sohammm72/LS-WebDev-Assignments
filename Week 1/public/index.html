Introduction

Hi, my name is Soham Murudkar. I am currently pursuing a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering at IIT Bombay. I am in my 2nd year of study and thoroughly enjoying the journey so far. Welcome to my digital scrapbook! Here I am sharing some of my bits of my hostel life with you!

First Year Hostel

I got Hostel 1 in my first year. Well I knew that the rooms of the hostels were not going to be small. But little did I know that these rooms were still much better than the ones I was going to get in my further years of study. But still it had its own perks like a very good mess, and a whole lot of good friends. I will always remember my "last wing last floor" room.

A little fun memory

Within the first few weeks, we had a little H1 v/s H16 face off.

First Mood Indigo

MOOD INDIGO was the biggest thing that we were all waiting for and boy was that a crazy experience for us in the first year itself. I could not attend all the 4 days but the 2 days that I enjoyed, I cannot forget. Enjoying the fun games with friends, and unexpectedly getting to listen to my favourite artist in the concert... just beautiful.

Second Year Hostel

Well in the first year I got the less nicer hostel among the two options available. But in my second year guess what... I got the worst one among the possible options. Welcome to Hostel 6, "Vikings". I do not know Worst part of this hostel was that I did not have any of my close friends with me in the same hostel. I do not know how I managed to survive 2 semesters.

Fun Fact

I have never tried the chicken on H6 Mess

Third Year Hostel

Finally after a year full of living in the "worst hostel of IITB", I was finally alloted one of the best hostels in IITB, that is Hostel 17. Thanks to the fast constructions of new 3 hostels, we got to escape our parent hostels and move into single-occupany rooms!
